图像超分辨率（SR）是计算机视觉领域的一个重要的研究课题，旨在从低分辨率（LR）图像恢复高分辨率（HR）图像。目前人们会因为图像采集设备的限制，会采集到细节丢失，视觉效果差的低分辨率图像。超分辨率技术就是要解决这些问题的，要做的不仅仅是指把图像的尺寸放大，更重要的是提升图像的清晰度，突出图像中的纹理和细节，改善视觉体验。

- **Detail Loss**: Low-resolution images lack enough pixels to display details, making small objects and textures difficult to recognize.
- **Poor Visual Quality**: Due to low resolution, images appear blurry and unclear, affecting user experience.
- **Information Loss**: Low-resolution images may not provide sufficient information, impacting image analysis and processing.



-Due to the limitations of image acquisition devices, people often obtain low-resolution images with lost details and poor visual quality. The goal of super-resolution technology is not only to enlarge the image size but more importantly, to enhance image clarity, highlight textures and details within the image, and improve the overall visual experience.
